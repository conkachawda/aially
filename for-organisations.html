The 5-Phase Engagement Framework

  • 1. Discover — Map current workflows, interview staff, identify repetitive and time-draining tasks. (Days 1–3)
  • 2. Diagnose — Score opportunities by impact and ease. Shortlist the top 3–5 quick wins. (Days 4–5)
  • 3. Deploy — Set up and configure recommended AI tools. Build prompts, templates, and standard operating procedures. (Weeks 2–4)
  • 4. Train — Run workshops for your staff. One-on-one coaching for power users. Written and video guides. (Weeks 3–6)
  • 5. Handover — Final report with measurable outcomes, tools deployed, staff trained, and a 90-day roadmap. (Final week)
