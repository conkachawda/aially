FAQ

Is this really free?

The bootcamp and certification are free. You get paid when you land a placement.

What if I can't find a placement?

Most Allies find their placement within 3 weeks of certifying. Our Pitch Kit gives you everything you need. If you're stuck, we run weekly coaching calls.

How much work is it?

Bootcamp: 2 days. Placement: typically 2 days/week for 4–10 weeks. You choose what fits around uni.

I'm in arts/law/nursing — is this for me?

Yes. AI is now a core skill in every field. Our Allies come from every faculty. In fact, non-technical backgrounds often get the best client outcomes because they speak human.

What's the catch?

Honestly, there isn't one. We make money when you land a placement. If you do well, we do well. The incentive is aligned.
